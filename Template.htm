Google Apps and Digital Citizenship
  
As students and teachers at CMS experienced early dismissals and cancelations in traditional learning, Google announced advances in their learning resources.
This week, Google has revealed their new site Google For Education. This site is a resource for all K12 educators and students to quickly access all of Google's products in the context of learning and teaching. A few of the things you will find include:
  • Training: Learn how to use Google Apps, become a Google Certified Educator, and connect with other educators using Google products!
  • Programs: Discover all the innovative projects Google is working on to create transformative educational resources. You'll find things like Maker Camp, Google in Code, and Google Education On Air
  • Tools and Solutions: Find extensive resources on Digital Citizenship, Collaboration, Search Services, and much more!
